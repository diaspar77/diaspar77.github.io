Vesper (2022) - Kristina Buozyte, Bruno Samper

Since watching Cronenberg's eXistenZ I longed to see more depictions of 'synthetic biology', the idea that biological/genetic engineering will eventually replace electronics. Vesper is a quite watchable, wonderfully weird and aesthetically interesting apocalyptic sci-fi (cf. Hunger Games) which does emits some unmistakably Cronenbergian vibes. Rosy McEwan's performance as Camellia and Vesper's greenhouse-lab of luminous dancing flowers stand out.
